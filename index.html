The Movement

Born from Scotland

ROCK.SCOT isn't just a radio station. We're a movement. The sound of a nation that lives and breathes rock music — from the Barrowlands to Belladrum, from Largs to Lochaber.

Born from the belief that Scotland deserves its own dedicated rock platform, we're here to amplify every riff, every drumbeat, every voice that makes Scottish rock culture what it is.

From garage bands to stadium legends, from underground metal to anthemic punk — we celebrate it all. Broadcasting 24/7 on DAB+ digital radio, uncompromising and unapologetic.

We launched in Inverclyde and North Ayrshire. But that's just the beginning. Our vision? All of Scotland.

One Scotland. One Rock Station. Unstoppable.

Station Identity

ROCK.SCOT is Scotland's dedicated DAB+ rock radio station, operated by Caledonia TX Ltd under Ofcom licence SC646223. It broadcasts 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, playing Metal, Alternative, Punk, Classic Rock and Grunge. It is the only station in Scotland focused exclusively on rock music.

Coverage Area

Currently live on DAB+ in Inverclyde (Greenock, Gourock, Paisley, Helensburgh) and North Ayrshire (Largs, Ardrossan, Saltcoats, Irvine, Kilwinning, Beith). South Lanarkshire and Oban licences confirmed, launching 2025/26. Total potential reach across licensed areas: 410,000+ people.

How to Listen

Search ROCK.SCOT on any DAB+ digital radio. Also available to stream live at rock.scot or via the player on this page. Free, no app required, available 24/7 worldwide.

Common Questions

What is ROCK.SCOT?

▾

ROCK.SCOT is Scotland's only dedicated 24/7 rock music radio station, broadcasting on DAB+ digital radio. Operating under Ofcom licence SC646223 by Caledonia TX Ltd, it plays Metal, Alternative, Punk and Classic Rock with a focus on Scottish artists and the wider rock community. To listen, search ROCK.SCOT on any DAB+ radio or stream online.

Where can I hear ROCK.SCOT on DAB+?

▾

ROCK.SCOT is currently live on DAB+ digital radio in Inverclyde (covering Greenock, Gourock, Helensburgh, Paisley and surrounding areas) and North Ayrshire (covering Largs, Ardrossan, Saltcoats, Irvine, Kilwinning and surrounding areas). South Lanarkshire coverage is licensed and coming in 2025/26. Search ROCK.SCOT on your DAB+ radio to find us.

What music does ROCK.SCOT play?

▾

ROCK.SCOT plays Rock, Metal, Alternative, Punk and Classic Rock — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The station champions Scottish rock acts alongside global greats. Programming includes Morning Rock with Tom Russell (6AM–10AM), The Rock Mid (10AM–2PM), Afternoon Drive (2PM–6PM), Evening Rock (6PM–10PM) and The Night Shift (10PM–6AM).

Who presents on ROCK.SCOT?

▾

Tom Russell presents Morning Rock on ROCK.SCOT, broadcasting live 6AM–10AM daily. Tom is a veteran Scottish broadcaster with over 20 years on air, previously at Real Radio, and is one of Scotland's most respected rock music presenters.
