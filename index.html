The Movement Begins

ROCK.SCOT isn't just a radio station. We're a movement. We're the sound of a nation that lives and breathes rock music.

Born from the belief that Scotland deserves its own dedicated rock platform, we're here to amplify every riff, every drum beat, every voice that makes Scottish rock culture what it is.

From garage bands to stadium legends, from underground metal to anthemic punk—we celebrate it all. Broadcasting 24/7 on DAB+ digital radio, we bring you an uncompromising mix that respects the music and the listeners.

We launched in Inverclyde, North Ayrshire, and South Lanarkshire. But that's just the beginning. Our vision? All of Scotland. We're expanding, growing, and building something that will echo across every corner of this nation.

This is Scotland's rock station. This is ROCK.SCOT.

PURE. RAW. SCOTTISH. ROCK.

TOM RUSSELL

The Voice Behind The Movement

Tom Russell is a broadcasting legend—a voice that's defined Scottish rock radio for over two decades. From Real Radio to Clyde 1, Tom has introduced generations to the music that matters.

His knowledge runs deep, his passion runs deeper, and his commitment to rock is unshakeable. Now he's joined ROCK.SCOT to help build something extraordinary.

A station that puts rock first, respects its audience, and showcases the incredible talent Scotland produces.

20+ years on air. Countless interviews. One mission: Pure rock.
