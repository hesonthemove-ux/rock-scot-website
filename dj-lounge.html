Broadcasting Responsibilities
You must acknowledge these responsibilities before accessing the DJ Lounge
Ofcom Broadcast Standards

As a broadcaster on ROCK.SCOT you are subject to Ofcom's Broadcasting Code. All content must comply with standards on harm and offence, accuracy, and fairness. Violations may result in formal investigation and sanctions against the station's licence.

Language & Content

Strong language, profanity, and offensive material are strictly prohibited at all times. ROCK.SCOT broadcasts 24/7 on DAB+ and must be suitable for all audiences. There are no watershed exemptions on digital radio.

Political Impartiality

You must not express personal political opinions on air, advocate for any political party, candidate, or campaign, or present political content in a way that is not duly impartial. This applies equally to local, national, and international politics.

Harm Avoidance

Content that could encourage or incite crime, lead to public disorder, or cause physical, mental, moral or social harm to listeners must never be broadcast. This includes material glorifying self-harm, drug use, or violence.

Accuracy & Fairness

News and factual content must be accurate and presented with due impartiality. Do not broadcast unverified information as fact. If you are unsure whether something is appropriate to broadcast, do not broadcast it — contact the station manager first.

Emergency Protocols

In the event of a major national incident (death of a senior Royal, national emergency, etc.) cease normal programming immediately and contact the station manager. Do not speculate on air. Default to the station's emergency playlist until instructed otherwise. Emergency contact details are in the Broadcasting Handbook.

Sponsorship & Advertising

Sponsored content must be clearly identified as such. Read sponsor scripts as provided — do not ad-lib around commercial content. Do not endorse products or services beyond what has been agreed with the station.

Confidentiality

Station operational details, staff information, and commercial arrangements are confidential. Do not discuss internal station matters on air or on personal social media channels in a way that could bring ROCK.SCOT into disrepute.
