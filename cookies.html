Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 8 February 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Caledonia TX Ltd trading as ROCK.SCOT uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, in compliance with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and analyze how you use the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Functions: Making the website work properly
  • Analytics: Understanding how visitors use our site
  • Player Persistence: Keeping the audio player active while you browse
  • Preferences: Remembering your cookie choices

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Allowed)

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences 1 year
rock_session_id Manages website session Session (deleted when browser closes)
player_state Keeps player running while browsing Session

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional - Requires Consent)

These help us understand how you use our website:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
rock_visitor_id Identifies unique visitors (anonymized) 2 years
_ga Google Analytics - visitor tracking 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - session data 2 years

What we track:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • How you arrived at our site (referrer)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop/tablet)
  • General location (country/region only)

What we DON'T track:

  • Your identity or personal information
  • Specific songs you listen to
  • Detailed browsing history outside our site

3.3 Local Storage (Not Cookies, But Similar)

We also use browser local storage for:

  • rock_visitor_id: Persistent visitor ID (if analytics enabled)
  • rock_session_start: Session timing
  • rock_page_count: Pages viewed this session

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Supabase (Database Services)

If you use our advertising booking system:

  • Purpose: User authentication and data storage
  • Provider: Supabase Inc. (EU/US servers)
  • Duration: Session + 7 days

4.2 Broadcast.Radio (Streaming Player)

When you use our live player:

  • Purpose: Stream delivery and playback
  • Provider: Broadcast.Radio Ltd (UK)
  • Duration: Session only

5. Legal Basis

5.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest (PECR Regulation 6(4))
We don't need consent for cookies essential to provide the service you requested.

5.2 Analytics Cookies

Legal Basis: Consent (PECR Regulation 6(1))
We ask for your explicit consent before setting non-essential cookies.

6. Your Cookie Choices

6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit, you'll see a cookie banner asking for consent. You can:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies including analytics
  • Reject Optional: Only essential cookies
  • Customize: Choose which cookie categories to allow

6.2 Change Your Mind

You can change preferences anytime:

6.3 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Warning: Blocking all cookies may prevent the website from working properly.

7. Cookie Retention

We follow PECR maximum retention periods:

  • Essential cookies: As long as needed for function
  • Analytics cookies: Maximum 26 months
  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser

8. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect "Do Not Track" browser signals. If DNT is enabled, we will:

  • Not set non-essential cookies
  • Not track your activity
  • Still provide full functionality

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you are under 16, please do not use our services without parental consent.
