Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 8 February 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site. We also use similar technologies like localStorage and sessionStorage.

2. What We Use

We keep our cookie use minimal and transparent. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking networks, or social media pixels.

NameTypePurposeDuration
rs-cookie-consent Essential Stores your cookie consent choice so we don't ask again on every visit 1 year (localStorage)
rs_visitor_id Analytics Anonymous visitor identifier for traffic counting. Does not identify you personally. 1 year (localStorage) — Consent required
rs_session_id Analytics Anonymous session identifier to count browsing sessions and pages per visit Session (sessionStorage) — Consent required
rockscot-theme Essential Remembers your display preference if applicable 1 year (localStorage)

3. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies are only placed if you click "Accept All" on our cookie banner. We use our own first-party analytics (via Supabase) rather than Google Analytics or third-party tools. This means:

  • Your data stays on our servers — not shared with advertising platforms
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No profiling or behavioural advertising
  • Data is aggregated and anonymised after 90 days

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our live player is embedded from Broadcast.Radio (player.broadcast.radio). This may set their own cookies. We do not control these — please refer to Broadcast.Radio's own privacy policy for details.

Google Fonts is used to load our typefaces. Google may log the request but does not set cookies for font loading.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies in several ways:

  • Our banner: Choose "Essential Only" to decline analytics cookies
  • Withdraw consent: Clear localStorage in your browser (DevTools → Application → Local Storage → delete rs-cookie-consent)
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies entirely

Note: Essential cookies cannot be disabled as the site requires them to function.
