Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 8 February 2026

1. Who We Are

Caledonia TX Ltd (trading as ROCK.SCOT), registered in Scotland (Company No. SC646223), VAT No. GB491589639. We operate Scotland's dedicated DAB+ rock radio station at rock.scot.

We are the Data Controller for all personal data processed under this policy.

Contact: info@rock.scot

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number and company name (via advertising enquiry forms)
  • Message content and campaign requirements
  • Discount codes used

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Pages visited and time spent on site (with your consent)
  • Device type and browser information
  • Referring website (where you came from)
  • Session data — anonymised visitor identifiers

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment card details (payments handled by bank transfer or Revolut)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks or retargeting cookies

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Advertising enquiries: To respond to your booking request, issue invoices and manage campaigns (Legitimate Interest / Contract performance)
  • Analytics: To understand how our website is used and improve it (Consent — only if you accept cookies)
  • Legal obligations: VAT records, financial compliance (Legal obligation)

4. Legal Bases (UK GDPR)

  • Article 6(1)(b) — Contract performance: processing enquiries and invoicing
  • Article 6(1)(c) — Legal obligation: VAT and financial records
  • Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interest: fraud prevention, site security
  • Article 6(1)(a) — Consent: analytics and non-essential cookies

5. Cookies

We use cookies only with your consent (except for essential security cookies). See our Cookie Policy for full details.

6. Data Retention

  • Advertising enquiries and customer data: 6 years (legal requirement for financial records)
  • Analytics data: 90 days rolling
  • Wire news: 30 days

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion where we have no legal obligation to retain
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time for consent-based processing

To exercise any right: info@rock.scot

8. Third Parties

  • Supabase Inc. — Database hosting (EU servers, UK GDPR compliant, DPA in place)
  • Broadcast.Radio / Caledonia TX — Streaming infrastructure
  • We do not share your data with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

9. Complaints

You have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or call 0303 123 1113.

We'd appreciate the opportunity to resolve any concerns directly first — please contact us at info@rock.scot.
