CAPACITY EXPLAINED

ROCK.SCOT operates on 3 DAB+ multiplexes — Inverclyde, North Ayrshire, and South Lanarkshire. Total system capacity is 120 units.

Single Mux — Weight 1. One advertiser on one mux. Uses 1 capacity unit. Max 120 single slots available.

Multi-Regional — Weight 3. Advertiser broadcasts across all 3 muxes simultaneously. Uses 3 capacity units. Max 40 multi slots available.

Top of Hour — Premium slot. Only 1 advertiser at a time. Cannot be combined with other TOH bookings.

The capacity widget shows remaining units in real time. Green = plenty of space. Amber = getting full. Red = nearly sold out. Never confirm a booking that would exceed capacity.

FINANCIAL DASHBOARD

Pipeline Value — Total value of all pending enquiries. This is potential revenue not yet confirmed.

Confirmed Revenue — Value of all confirmed and live campaigns. Money you can count on.

Completed Revenue — Campaigns that have fully run. Realised income.

Total Booked — All non-cancelled bookings combined (pending + confirmed + live + completed). Your gross pipeline.

ADMIN NOTES

Every lead has a private notes log — visible only in this admin panel, never to the client. Use it to track every interaction: calls made, emails sent, creative received, payment chased, etc.

Notes are timestamped with your email and the exact date/time automatically. They're stored in order oldest to newest so you can read the full story of a booking from top to bottom.

A 📝 badge on a lead in the table shows how many notes it has, so you can quickly see which leads have active correspondence.
